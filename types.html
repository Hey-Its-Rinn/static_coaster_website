Coaster Types

The construction materials used to build coller coasters are wood and steel. Coasters can be built primarily out of wood or out of steel, but some coaster designs use both materials. In general, the material the track is built out of determines how a coaster is classified. There are steel-tracked coasters on wooden supports and there are wood-tracked coasters on steel latices. There are also hybrid coasters, which blur the line by forming steel track in the traditional shape of wooden coasters.
