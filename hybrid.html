Hybrid Coasters

Rocky Mountain Construction has a proven track record of bringing new life to old wooden coasters by transforming them into hybrids. Steel Vengence is a prime example. Notice how the track is not like a traditional steel coaster. Instead it resembles that of a wooden coaster. The steel allows for elements not possible on a pure-wood design, but by keeping the profile the same. When retrofitting a ride, they can preserve the overall experience and then add to it by taking advantage of the materials strength to modify the layout and add even wilder elements than the ride had previously.
