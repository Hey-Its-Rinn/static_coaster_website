Wooden Coasters

The Voyage is special to me because it is the big ride at Holiday World, which is the park I travelled to almost yearly. When I ride a wooden roller coaster, I always compare it to this one. It's built by Gravity Group. What meakes it an incredible ride is how it begins with 3 big hills, not unlike a tall steel mega coaster. It then then has a wicked turnaround before spending the rest of the ride traveling downhill, going faster and faster, very low to the ground. The ride is ROUGH. The front row and the last row are also completely different rides. Sit in the front for an amazing view charging through the forest, or sit in the back for ejector-seat airtime as the train whips over the top of each and every hill.
